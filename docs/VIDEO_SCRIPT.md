# VIDEO WALKTHROUGH SCRIPT
## Best Astrologer in Dandenong - Website Demo
**Total Duration: 4-5 minutes**

---

## INTRO (15 seconds)
**SHOW:** Homepage hero section

**SAY:**
> "Hi, I'm excited to show you your new professional website for Best Astrologer in Dandenong. This site is designed to work for you 24/7 - bringing in bookings, answering customer questions, and making your business look as credible as your 248 five-star reviews deserve. Let me show you how it all works."

---

## SECTION 1: HOMEPAGE (45 seconds)
**SHOW:** Scroll through homepage slowly

**SAY:**
> "First, the homepage. Notice the stunning mystical design that immediately tells visitors they're dealing with a professional. Your 248 five-star reviews are highlighted right at the top - that's powerful social proof.

> As we scroll down, visitors see all four of your services laid out beautifully - Psychic Reading, Astrology Consultation, Love Reading, and Spiritual Healing. Each one links directly to booking.

> Here are real testimonials from satisfied clients. This builds trust before they even speak to you.

> And notice this purple chat bubble in the corner? That's your 24/7 AI assistant - we'll come back to that."

---

## SECTION 2: BOOKING SYSTEM (60 seconds)
**SHOW:** Click "Book a Reading" → Select a service → Pick a date → Choose time → Fill form

**SAY:**
> "Now let's look at how easy it is for customers to book. They click 'Book a Reading' and get this beautiful 3-step process.

> Step 1: They choose their service. Each shows the duration so they know what to expect.

> Step 2: They pick a date from the calendar and select an available time slot. The system automatically hides times that are already booked - no double bookings, ever.

> Step 3: They enter their details. Notice there's space for them to write what they want to discuss - so you're prepared before they arrive.

> When they submit, you get notified instantly in your admin dashboard. No more back-and-forth phone calls or missed messages."

---

## SECTION 3: AI CHAT ASSISTANT (45 seconds)
**SHOW:** Click chat bubble → Type a question → Show AI response

**SAY:**
> "Here's something special - your AI assistant. It's available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, answering customer questions even while you sleep.

> Let me ask it something... 'What services do you offer?'

> See how it responds naturally? It knows about your business - your location, your hours, your services. It can answer common questions about what to expect from a reading.

> If someone has a complex question the AI can't handle, it directs them to your contact form or phone number. This means fewer missed enquiries and more booked appointments."

---

## SECTION 4: CONTACT PAGE (20 seconds)
**SHOW:** Navigate to Contact page

**SAY:**
> "For specific enquiries, there's a dedicated contact page. Your phone number, address, and business hours are clearly displayed, plus an embedded Google Map so people can find you easily.

> The contact form captures their details and saves them directly to your admin dashboard."

---

## SECTION 5: BLOG (30 seconds)
**SHOW:** Navigate to Blog → Click an article → Scroll through

**SAY:**
> "Now here's something that will help you rank on Google - a full blog section with professionally written articles about each of your services.

> These aren't just any articles - they're SEO-optimised to help people searching for 'psychic reading Dandenong' or 'astrologer Melbourne' find YOUR website. 

> Each article builds trust by educating readers and ends with a call to book - turning readers into clients."

---

## SECTION 6: ADMIN DASHBOARD (90 seconds)
**SHOW:** Navigate to Admin → Login → Show dashboard → Click through tabs

**SAY:**
> "Now let me show you where the magic happens - your admin dashboard. This is your private control centre.

> Login is simple and secure. Once inside, you see everything at a glance - total bookings, pending appointments, new enquiries, and even how many AI chat conversations happened this week.

> Let's look at the Bookings tab. Every booking appears here with the client's details. You can change the status - pending, confirmed, completed, or cancelled.

> Here's a great feature - click any booking card and it flips over to show you the client's contact details. One tap to call them, text them, or email them. And see this notes section? Add private notes about each client for next time.

> And notice this sidebar? You can collapse it for more screen space, or expand it when you need the navigation."

---

**SAY:**
> "So that's your new website - professional design that matches your reputation, automatic booking that saves you hours, an AI assistant that works around the clock, SEO-optimised content that brings in new clients, and a dashboard that keeps you in control.

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Pace yourself** - Speak slowly and clearly, pause between sections
2. **Use your mouse** - Point at features as you describe them
3. **Keep it natural** - It's okay to slightly deviate from the script
4. **Record in one take if possible** - Edits can feel choppy
5. **Background music** - Add soft, mystical music in post (optional)

## PAGES TO SHOW IN ORDER
1. `/` - Homepage (hero, services, testimonials)
2. `/booking` - Complete a booking flow
3. Homepage - Click chat widget, ask a question
4. `/contact` - Show form and map
5. `/blog` - Show articles
6. `/admin` - Login and demo dashboard
7. `/` - End on homepage

## KEY BENEFITS TO EMPHASISE
- 24/7 availability (AI chat + online booking)
- Professional credibility (design matches 5-star reputation)
- Time savings (no phone tag, automated scheduling)
- Never miss a client (all enquiries tracked)
- Google visibility (SEO blog content)
- Easy management (simple admin dashboard)
